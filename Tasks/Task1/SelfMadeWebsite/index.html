About Us - The Curator's Vision

Preserving Nature's
Fleeting Moments

The Feathered Frame is more than a gallery; it is a sanctuary for the soaring spirits of the avian world. We believe that every feather tells a story of survival, freedom, and grace.

Founded in 2026, our mission is to connect collectors with artwork that breathes. From the silent vigil of the owl to the vibrant dart of the hummingbird, our curated pieces are selected for their ability to bring the wild outdoors into the intimacy of your home.

Exhibitions

15 November 2026

Wings of Stillness

Opening on Saturday, 15th November, this exhibition celebrates serenity, purity, and quiet strength through a curated collection of avian artworks. Centered around the white pigeon — a timeless symbol of peace and grace — the exhibition brings together refined paintings and sculptures from distinguished wildlife artists.

Each piece captures stillness in motion, inviting viewers to pause and reflect on nature's most delicate expressions.

Join us from midday for an intimate viewing accompanied by light refreshments.

20 November 2026

Flight of Imagination

An evening exhibition inspired by imagination and movement, featuring a striking crested bird rendered through expressive form and texture. This collection explores the boundary between realism and artistic interpretation, where feathers become rhythm and flight becomes emotion.

Designed for those drawn to bold creativity, the exhibition transforms the gallery into a space of visual storytelling and contemporary wildlife expression.

The gallery remains open late, welcoming visitors into an atmospheric and festive setting.

Featured Collection

Guardians of the Sky

This sculptural showcase brings together the commanding presence of the eagle and the quiet wisdom of the owl. Crafted in fine detail, these works celebrate balance — strength and stillness, vigilance and grace.

Each sculpture reflects the artist's mastery of form and material, capturing the spirit of these birds beyond surface detail and into character and symbolism.
